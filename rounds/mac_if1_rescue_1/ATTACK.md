# MAC-IF1 isolated attack

## Scope

Attack the rescued `MAC-IF1` claim, not the historical uniform `MAC-BI1`. The proposal is a low-coverage weak-anionic shallow zone adjacent to exposed low-polarity pore wall on a millimeter porous St–DVB particle, intended for joint ROX/ERY/CLA/AZI removal.

## Critical findings

### A1. The biological observation and proposed microscopic cause are not yet causally linked

The 500 μm inactivated wastewater biofilm jointly enriched CLA, ERY and ROX, but the study did not localize the effect to EPS, lipids, pore-wall chemistry or a specific spatial organization. Independent phospholipid studies support shallow macrolide partition, yet they do not show that this mechanism causes the thick-biofilm class boundary. Combining the two literatures is a testable inference, not a demonstrated biological mechanism.

Disposition: critical and resolvable only through B0. The Introduction must keep the connection as a hypothesis until the interface-composition and matched-drug tests pass.

### A2. The same interfacial behavior occurs for unrelated cationic amphiphiles

Propranolol partitions strongly into anionic membranes and localizes near the polar headgroup region. Therefore negative charge plus a neighboring hydrophobic region is not a macrolide-specific biological solution. A material that removes all cationic amphiphiles equally cannot claim macrolide-class recognition.

Disposition: critical and resolvable only if B0 and C0 show a class-relevant response beyond charge and logD.

### A3. The proposed material family is crowded

MAn–St–DVB beads, millimeter polyDVB followed by maleic-acid functionalization, surface-carboxylated erythromycin resins, hydrophobic/WCX mixed-mode materials, thin functional shells and macrolide MIPs all exist. Photografting PAA is also established. Novelty cannot reside in composition, surface carboxylation, thin shell, or the words “mixed-mode”.

Disposition: critical. Only directly measured shallow spatial organization plus matched spatial-distribution causality remains defensible.

### A4. The primary manufacturing route may not make the claimed internal pore-wall structure

UV illumination can be nonuniform in an agitated bed; benzophenone and AA may remain near the exterior, diffuse through open pores, form free PAA, or block pore mouths. A fluorescent shell at the bead exterior would not prove nanoscale anionic–low-polarity adjacency throughout accessible pore walls.

Disposition: critical and resolvable only through M0 using multiple spatial and accessibility measurements. XPS alone is insufficient.

## High findings

### A5. Same-composition controls are difficult to match

Changing solvent swelling, irradiation time or graft coverage also changes acid density, pore volume and diffusion. A two-sample comparison will confound position with quantity. The experiment needs a matrix spanning acid amount and spatial distribution, followed by a model that tests their independent and interactive effects.

### A6. A dense PAA layer could destroy the proposed dual contact

A continuous water-swollen PAA film may screen the underlying hydrophobic surface and turn the material into an ordinary weak-acid gel. The design requires low coverage and exposed low-polarity area. A non-monotonic coverage–performance relation is therefore mandatory, not optional.

### A7. Secondary-effluent ions and DOM may erase the gain

Ca²⁺/Mg²⁺ and ionic strength can occupy or screen carboxylate sites, while DOM can cover hydrophobic surfaces or complex macrolides in solution. Pure-water success does not justify an engineering claim.

### A8. Simple chemistry does not guarantee low treatment cost

Rotating UV exposure, ethanol recovery, wash-water demand, graft yield and layer loss may dominate finished cost. The scale claim requires energy and solvent mass balance and comparison per cubic metre of treated water.

## Strongest skeptical interpretation

An informed reviewer could say that `MAC-IF1` is an old surface-carboxylated mixed-mode resin with a membrane analogy added after design. The only adequate rebuttal is experimental: the biological rule must make a prediction about spatial position, the material must realize that position, and moving the same chemistry away from that position must reduce the target-class function in realistic water.

## Attack verdict

`revise / conditional lead / not E1-ready`. No issue is hidden by a numerical score. A1–A4 remain critical until B0/M0/C0 evidence exists.
